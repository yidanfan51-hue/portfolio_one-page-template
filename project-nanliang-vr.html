Nanliang Red Culture VR Exhibition

VR Experience / Digital Heritage / National Art Fund Project

Project Video

Research Overview

Supported by the National Art Fund, this project focuses on the digital preservation and virtual translation of Nanliang’s red cultural heritage. It aims to reconstruct historical narratives within a high-fidelity immersive space, balancing academic rigor with interactive engagement.

Technical Framework

  • Engine: Unity 3D
  • Hardware: HTC VIVE Pro System
  • Interaction: Ray-casting pointer for real-time textual interpretation.
  • UX Optimization: Integrated instant teleportation and dynamic FOV reduction to mitigate cyber-sickness.
