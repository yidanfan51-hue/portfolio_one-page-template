Nanliang Red Culture VR Exhibition

A National Art Fund Supported Project

This project focuses on the digital preservation and virtual translation of Nanliang’s red cultural heritage. It aims to reconstruct historical narratives within a high-fidelity immersive space, balancing academic rigor with interactive engagement.

Technical Specifications
  • Engine: Unity
  • Hardware: HTC VIVE Pro System
  • Interaction: Ray-casting pointer for real-time textual interpretation of oil paintings.
  • Locomotion: Integrated instant teleportation and dynamic field-of-view (FOV) reduction to mitigate cyber-sickness and ensure physiological comfort.

By balancing technical performance with ergonomic interaction, this research demonstrates a standardized approach to the digital curation of cultural memory.
