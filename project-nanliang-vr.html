Nanliang Red Culture VR Exhibition

A National Art Fund Supported Project

Technical Framework
  • Platform: Unity 3D
  • Hardware: HTC VIVE Pro System
  • Logic: Ray-casting pointer interaction
  • UX Strategy: Instant teleportation & Dynamic FOV reduction to mitigate cyber-sickness.

This research investigates the digital preservation and virtual translation of red cultural heritage. By utilizing immersive technology, the project reconstructs historical narratives within a high-fidelity spatial environment.

The system prioritizes embodied spatial exploration and physiological comfort, establishing a standardized methodological framework for the digital curation of cultural memory.

Keywords: Virtual Reality, Digital Humanities, Locomotion Interaction, Cultural Heritage
