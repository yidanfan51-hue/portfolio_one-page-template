Overview

Endless Body is a VR experience exploring embodied perception through a continuously transforming virtual organism. Users are placed within an immersive environment where perception is shaped through motion, scale shifts, and synchronized audiovisual rhythms.

Concept Development

This board documents the visual research and development process behind Endless Body, including character studies, environmental references, natural forms, animation testing, and scene construction.
