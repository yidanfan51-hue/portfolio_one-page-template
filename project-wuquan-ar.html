From Passive Viewing to Active Exploration: Designing an AR-Based Park Experience

AR Experience / Location-Based Narrative

Context

This project investigates Wuqian Mountain Park as a layered spatial environment, where historical memory, everyday activities, and cultural narratives coexist. Through mapping and analysis, the project identifies opportunities to embed interactive digital experiences into physical space.

Field Research

On-site observations and behavioral mapping reveal how visitors navigate, pause, and interact with the park environment. These insights inform the design of interaction points and narrative triggers.

Problem Framing

Despite its cultural richness, the park experience remains largely passive. Key tensions include fragmented storytelling, lack of engagement, and limited connection between users and spatial narratives.

Concept Development

The design proposes an AR-based system that overlays digital narratives onto physical locations, transforming exploration into an interactive journey.

Prototype

A functional prototype demonstrates how users engage with location-based tasks, unlock stories, and interact with the environment through mobile interfaces.
