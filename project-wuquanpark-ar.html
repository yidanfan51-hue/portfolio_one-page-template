Overview

This AR game project at Wuquan Mountain Park reimagines traditional park exploration through a location-based interactive experience. By embedding digital narratives and game tasks into specific physical sites, users are guided to discover cultural landmarks, natural scenery, and historical stories in an immersive way.

Through playful interaction and spatial storytelling, the project transforms passive sightseeing into an active journey, enhancing users’ sense of place, memory, and social connection within the park.
